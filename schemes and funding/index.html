Schemes and Services

Champion Services Sector Scheme:

The Champion Services Sector Scheme have been framed by Govt. of India to enhance Medical tourism in the country. A Central Sector Scheme for the development of Ayush entrepreneurship.
Incubation Centre for Innovation Entrepreneurship:

An incubation center set up by AIIA to support new startups in the Ayush sector. Ministry of Ayush has taken several initiatives to encourage a startup culture.
Central Sector Scheme for Promotion of Ayush Intervention in Public Health Initiatives (PHI):

A scheme that provides grants to government and non-government organizations to roll out proven Ayush interventions.

Funding schemes

Ayurswasthya

The AYURSWASTHYA Yojana, financial assistance is provided to eligible individual organizations for establishing and upgrading their functions for research & development activities.

Ayurgyan

AYURGYAN Scheme has been approved for promoting education and research in the field of Ayush and to support Research & Innovation in Ayush research and education in Ayush.
Seed Fund Scheme

Financial assistance to startups for proof of concept, prototype development, product trials, market entry,that ensure the policy of the AYUSH department and commercialization.
